Q: Is English commonly spoken on Taniti Island?

A: English is fluently spoken by younger generations and is common in Tanity City. English is uncommon in rural areas and rare among older natives.

Q: What is the drinking age in Taniti?

A: The drinking age is 18 in Taniti. The age limit is not strictly enforced.
